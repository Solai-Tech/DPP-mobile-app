CirTag Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Introduction

CirTag ("we", "our", or "us") is a Digital Product Passport platform developed by Solai Tech. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the CirTag mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access: We access your device camera solely to scan QR codes on products. Camera images are processed locally and are not stored or transmitted.

Product Data: When you scan a product, we retrieve product information from our servers to display the Digital Product Passport.

Chat Data: Messages you send through the in-app chatbot are stored locally on your device and may be sent to our servers to provide support responses.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type and operating system version for app compatibility purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display Digital Product Passport information for scanned products
  • Provide customer support through the in-app chatbot
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage

Product scan history and chat messages are stored locally on your device using SQLite database. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for chatbot functionality. These services process your chat messages to provide responses. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
